# Archer Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 1, 2026

**LANGUAGE NOTICE:** This document may be translated into other languages. In the event of any conflict or inconsistency between the English version and any translated version, the English version shall prevail.

**DISCLAIMER:** This document is a draft and requires review by qualified legal professionals before use.

---

Atharch Technology Ltd. ("Atharch," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Archer mobile application ("App" or "Archer").

By using Archer, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these practices, please do not use Archer.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- Email address
- Firebase user identifier (a unique ID assigned to your account for authentication purposes)
- Password (stored only as a secure hash; we never store or have access to your actual password)
- Account preferences and settings

### 1.2 Query Data

When you use Archer to query Knowledge Bases:

- Your queries are transmitted to our servers for processing
- Query results are returned to your device
- Query history may be cached temporarily for performance purposes

### 1.3 Local Device Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

- Chat history and conversation logs
- Cached query results
- App preferences and settings
- Access Key information

This local data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless necessary for providing the service.

### 1.4 Device Information

We may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- App version
- Language and locale settings
- Time zone

### 1.5 Usage Data

We collect information about your use of Archer, including:

- Permits consumption statistics
- Feature usage patterns
- Error logs and crash reports
- Session duration and frequency

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

### 2.1 Provide Query Services

- Process your queries and return results from Knowledge Bases
- Authenticate your access to Knowledge Bases via Access Keys
- Track and manage your Permits allocation

### 2.2 Improve Our Services

- Analyze usage patterns to enhance Archer's functionality
- Identify and fix bugs and technical issues
- Develop new features and improvements

### 2.3 Security and Fraud Prevention

- Protect against unauthorized access to your account
- Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity
- Enforce our Terms of Service

### 2.4 Communications

- Send you important notices about your account or the service
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests

---

## 3. Information Sharing

### 3.1 Google Cloud Platform Services

Archer uses Google Cloud Platform services to process queries and provide functionality. Specifically:

- **Google Gemini AI:** Your queries are sent to Google Gemini AI for processing. The AI analyzes your query against the relevant Knowledge Base content and generates a response, which is then returned to your device. Query data sent to Gemini AI is processed in accordance with Google's data handling practices.

- **Firebase Services:** We use Firebase for user authentication, data storage, and cloud functions.

- **Google Cloud Storage:** Documents uploaded to Knowledge Bases are stored in Google Cloud Storage.

All data processing involving Google services is governed by Google's privacy policies and terms of service. By using Archer, you acknowledge that your data may be processed by Google Cloud Platform in accordance with their data handling practices.

For more information, please review:
- Google Cloud Privacy Notice: https://cloud.google.com/terms/cloud-privacy-notice
- Google Cloud Terms of Service: https://cloud.google.com/terms

### 3.2 Knowledge Base Owners

We may share anonymized and aggregated usage statistics with Knowledge Base Owners, such as:

- Total number of queries to their Knowledge Base
- Permits consumption metrics
- General usage trends

**Important:** We do not share your actual query content or personal information with Knowledge Base Owners without your consent.

### 3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:

- Valid legal process (subpoena, court order, etc.)
- Requests from law enforcement or government agencies
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others
- To investigate suspected violations of our Terms of Service

### 3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you of any such change and any choices you may have regarding your information.

### 3.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you have given us your explicit consent to do so.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage Location

Data storage locations are determined by:

- **Google Cloud Platform:** Our backend infrastructure is hosted on Google Cloud Platform. Data storage and processing occur in accordance with Google's data center locations and practices.
- **Knowledge Base Owner Configuration:** Knowledge Base Owners may configure regional settings for their Knowledge Bases through their Google Cloud Project.
- **Your Device:** Chat history and cached data are stored locally on your device.

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

- Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL
- Secure password hashing
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments

Data processed through Google Cloud Platform is protected in accordance with Google's security standards and certifications.

### 4.3 Local Device Security

Data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's security features. We recommend:

- Using a strong device passcode
- Enabling device encryption
- Keeping your device's operating system updated

---

## 5. Your Rights

### 5.1 Account Deletion

You may delete your Archer account at any time through the App's settings or by contacting us. Upon account deletion:

- Your account information will be removed from our servers
- Your locally stored data will remain on your device until you delete it manually
- Some information may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes

### 5.2 Local Data Management

You can manage your locally stored data at any time:

- Clear your chat history through the App's settings
- Delete cached data
- Remove Access Keys

### 5.3 Knowledge Base Content

**Important Notice:** As a User, you do not have the right to access, correct, or delete Knowledge Base content. Knowledge Bases are owned and controlled by Knowledge Base Owners, who are solely responsible for their content. If you have concerns about Knowledge Base content, please contact the Knowledge Base Owner directly.

### 5.4 Access and Portability

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, please contact us using the information provided below.

### 5.5 Regional Rights

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR or CCPA). To exercise any such rights, please contact us.

---

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Query Cache

Query results may be temporarily cached for performance purposes. This cache is automatically cleared on a short-term basis (typically within hours to days).

### 6.2 Chat History

Your chat history is stored locally on your device. You control how long this data is retained and can clear it at any time through the App's settings.

### 6.3 Account Data

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. After account deletion:

- Most personal data is deleted promptly
- Some information may be retained for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or to resolve disputes
- Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained indefinitely

### 6.4 Usage Statistics

Anonymized usage statistics may be retained indefinitely for analytics and service improvement purposes.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Archer is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

We take appropriate safeguards to ensure that your information receives an adequate level of protection in the jurisdictions where we process it.
